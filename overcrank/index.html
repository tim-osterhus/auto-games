Operator notes

  • Move with A/D or Left/Right. Jump with Space, W, or Up.
  • Touch a wall while falling to slide, then jump to wall kick away.
  • Score comes from height gain. Keep climbing without stalling to extend the chain and multiply each rise.
  • Blue lifts move, amber catwalks crumble, and steam vents flash before they fire.
  • Coolant pickups push back the heat and slow the chase for a short window. If the heat line reaches you, or steam hits you, the run is over.
